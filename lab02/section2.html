Study & Data Methods

Fieldwork combined plot mapping, instrumentation, and data analysis to examine how gap size and position influence microclimate, seedling survival, and early growth. Each gap was mapped using Field-Map and GIS tools, recording tree coordinates, species, height, and survival status. Environmental loggers tracked soil moisture and temperature across north and south transects.

Workflow Overview

  • Plot design & mapping: Mark gap center and cardinal edges; collect GPS and canopy data.
  • Seedling survey: Record species, height, survival, and condition notes.
  • Environmental monitoring: Deploy sensors for soil moisture, light, and temperature.
  • Data processing: Merge field data in spreadsheets; export to GIS for visualization and analysis.
